Mary Shelly's House of Frankenstein

For those fascinated by Mary Shelley's life and works or just an avid Frankenstein enthusiast, Mary Shelley's House of Frankenstein is one of Baths must-visit attractions. Offering a plethora of experiences including Escape Rooms, "The Frankenstein Experience" walking tour, and the "Body In A Suitcase" Game, alongside general access to Mary Shelley House, there's something for everyone.
